Class set up for Coronavirus.

Entrance and Exit System

  1. All students to be dressed and ready waiting in car park 10 minutes before their class time.
  2. Students to line up at entrance door 2 m apart.
  3. Students use hand sanitiser before entering Hall.
  4. Students make their way into Hall and directly onto their training spot where they will stay for the whole session.
  5. Instructor to take attendance Register.
  6. At end of class students leave building via fire escape again using hand sanitiser as they leave.

PLEASE NOTE:

The only reason that we are allowed to operate is that it is a ‘supervised session’ and the Instructor is strictly bound to ensure that all students remain at least 2 meters apart, any student not following this guideline will be asked to leave the session (this includes anytime spent on the premises).

Masks/Face coverings need to be worn on entry and on exit of the class while traveling through the Building, please supply your own. (Face coverings do not need to be worn while training).

Could you please ensure that if you attend our classes you wear training foot wear, at all times, from now onwards during this epidemic.
Trainers, Plimpsoles, sports shoes and martial art shoes are all ideal, please no flip flops, crocs etc.

Any transactions that need to be made in class (Licence renewal, Competition entry, Equipment purchase etc) need to be placed in an envelope with the name of the student clearly written on the front and an explanation of its contents so it can be processed for the following week, this envelope needs to be sealed and placed on the ‘Drop Off / Pick Up Table’ when entering the Class please.

These required items can be picked up at the end of the class, of the following week at the ‘Drop Off / Pick Up Table’ on Exit.

No Training Holdalls in the Building Please.

Drinks and Toilet Breaks will cause a problem within the class as far as time lost in the session, disruption and movement within the training hall at this time of social distancing, please ensure you are well hydrated before Training, and that all students have visited their toilet at home before leaving for the Class. (However, Toilets will be Available at our training centres)
